Did You Know?

Some dinosaurs had feathers!

Many theropods, like the Velociraptor, had feathers — and birds today are actually considered modern dinosaurs.

The Stegosaurus had a brain the size of a walnut.

Despite its large body, its brain was only about the size of a lime!

Tyrannosaurus rex had one of the strongest bites in history.

Its bite force could crush bones — over 12,000 pounds of pressure!

The name "dinosaur" means "terrible lizard."

It was coined in 1842 by British scientist Richard Owen — but dinosaurs aren't actually lizards!
